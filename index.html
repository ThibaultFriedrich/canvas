Build a product really adapted to your customers.

The value proposition canvas is a great tool to make your product solves your customer's needs. We just make it easier to edit, easier to share, easier to print.

No pain, no gain !

Before building a great product, you need to find a pain to resolve or a gain to improve. Otherwise whatever cool is what your offer, you won't sell it.

Some smart guys have designed the value proposition canvas to synthetize your thoughs about your product/market fit.

Why our product?

Most of websites proposing value proposition canvas only give access to a picture or if you are lucky to a pdf. The edition is horrible. The sharing of the editable version is also a nightmare.

With our digital solution, everything is easy. Edition is easy. Export is easy. Printing is easy. You don't have anymore bad reasons to don't use the value proposition canvas.
